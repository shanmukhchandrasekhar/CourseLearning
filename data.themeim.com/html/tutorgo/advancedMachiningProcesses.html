Description

Advanced machining processes are the cutting-edge techniques revolutionizing manufacturing. From Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) to Laser Beam Machining (LBM), these methods go beyond traditional machining to achieve precision and efficiency. Discover how Non-Traditional Machining (NTM) methods like Ultrasonic Machining and Abrasive Water Jet Machining reshape metals with unparalleled accuracy. Dive into the world of High-Speed Machining (HSM) and Computer Numerical Control (CNC) for intricate designs and faster production rates. Explore the benefits of these processes, from reduced material waste to enhanced surface finish, driving the future of precision engineering.

This event will allow participants to:

  • Hands-On Experience with Cutting-Edge Technologies.
  • Explore the Efficiency of Non-Traditional Machining Methods
  • Master High-Speed Machining and CNC Techniques.
