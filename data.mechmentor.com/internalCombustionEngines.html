Internal combustion Engines.

Teacher

Murthy

Event date:

July 30, 2024

Location:

Aryabatta Block, Indian Naval Academy

Description

Internal combustion engines are the heartbeat of modern transportation, powering cars, trucks, and aircraft worldwide. Delve into the intricate mechanisms of these engines, where fuel and air combine in controlled explosions to generate power. Explore the evolution from simple two-stroke engines to sophisticated four-stroke designs, each revolutionizing efficiency and performance. Uncover the principles of combustion, ignition, and exhaust systems, essential for optimizing engine performance. From the iconic V8 engines to the eco-friendly hybrid models, discover how these marvels of engineering have shaped the automotive industry. Dive into topics like turbocharging, variable valve timing, and direct fuel injection, technologies that continue to push the boundaries of efficiency and power in internal combustion engines.

This event will allow participants to:

  • Understanding the Fundamentals of Combustion.
  • Exploring Engine Design: From Two-Stroke to Four-Stroke.
  • Unveiling Advanced Technologies: Turbocharging and Variable Valve Timing.
